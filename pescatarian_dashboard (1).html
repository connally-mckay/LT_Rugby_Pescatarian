üéØ WHEN TO SKIP FISH

Red Flags:

  • ‚úó Smells "fishy" or ammonia-like (should smell like ocean)
  • ‚úó Eyes are cloudy (whole fish - fresh = clear eyes)
  • ‚úó Flesh is mushy or discolored
  • ‚úó Sketchy restaurant kitchen
  • ‚úó Suspiciously cheap ($5.99/lb "salmon" = fraud)
  • ‚úó No wild/farmed label (they're hiding something)
  • ‚úó Already had tuna twice this week (mercury adds up)

üí° The Mercury Rule

  • Small fish (sardines, anchovies, mackerel) = almost no mercury
  • Medium fish (salmon, cod, trout) = low mercury
  • Big predator fish (tuna, swordfish) = mercury bombs
  • Mercury bioaccumulates up the food chain
  • For rugby: You need your brain and nervous system sharp
